Open Food Data

Sarah wants local fresh healthy food

looks for possible recipes according to her diet

picks up a set of recipes for the coming days

searches where she can find those products

chooses how to be delivered or picked-up

Tom likes to keep track on his eco foot print and be fair to Farmers

looks up for local short supply chain options

identifies local producers and supply chains

can check ecological and economic properties of those options

can choose to order online or buy from local store

Bob is a farmer and wants to sell locally and get more value for his produce

looks up for possible cooperation with other Food Actors

prepares a catalogue of produce he can offer

makes agreements with other Food Actors

Start to make commitments and sell locally

Hilde is an interpreter and want to help local economy

wants to create a FoodHub in her neighbourhood

looks for local farmers and other Food Actors

identify collaborators and makes agreements

runs and online food shop and shares with community

Peter has a refrigerated van and wants to feed his family and be useful to the society

Looks for FoodHubs and Farmer which needs help in transportation

identify collaborators and makes agreements

starts delivery

helps producers and consumers

How?

  • Provide Linked Open Quality community managed Food Data
  • Provide user friendly tools to work with this data
  • Enable linking current solutions and data-sets
  • Make people aware of what they buy and eat and how it affects the environment and the community
  • Convince big brands and retailers that this is for Good for them too
  • Enable Fair Food Supply Chain
